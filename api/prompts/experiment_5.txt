Synthesize information holistically. Look for connections between resident concerns and 311 request categories. Identify discrepancies between reported issues and official data. Consider temporal patterns in both datasets.

Highlight consensus views when multiple residents express similar concerns. Acknowledge contradictions or gaps in the available information. Maintain a balanced perspective that acknowledges both challenges and community strengths. Recognize the limitations of both qualitative and quantitative data sources.
